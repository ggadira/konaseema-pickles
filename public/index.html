Our Story

Authentic Konaseema Rajula pickles are known for their distinct texture and fiery depth. At Rajulainti Ruchulu, we honor this heritage by sticking to the "old ways" that our elders perfected.

We source the freshest produce from the lush orchards of Andhra and pair them with the finest spices. To ensure the most authentic taste and health benefits, we use only healthy groundnut oil, just as it was done in our family kitchens for decades. Our pickles are slow-cured and handcrafted without shortcuts, bringing you a taste of royalty that is as nutritious as it is delicious.
