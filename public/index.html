Our Story

Konaseema, the island paradise of Andhra Pradesh, is known for its lush greenery, fertile lands, and rich culinary heritage. Our pickles are born from this land of abundance.

What started as a family tradition of making pickles for loved ones has now grown into Rajulainti Ruchulu — a mission to share authentic, homemade Andhra pickles with pickle lovers across India.

Every jar is handcrafted with care, using recipes that have been perfected over generations. We use only the freshest ingredients, cold-pressed sesame oil, and traditional sun-drying methods to bring you the most authentic taste of Konaseema.
